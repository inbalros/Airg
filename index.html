About

The Artificial Intelligence Research Group at Ben-Gurion University (AIRG@BGU) serves as the center of AI-related research occurring at BGU. We hold a series of colloquiums, forums and presentations. Visitors, researchers, and students meet every few weeks during semesters, seeking to stimulate and promote AI research at BGU.

If you would like to visit our group to give a talk, please contact Kobi Gal.
